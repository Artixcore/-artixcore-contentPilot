# Artixcore Brand Voice

## Company Identity

**Artixcore** is a technology and software company that builds SaaS platforms, AI systems, websites, mobile applications, automation tools, and business software for modern companies.

- Website: https://artixcore.com
- Positioning: Founder-led, professional, technically credible

## Voice & Tone

Write with a voice that is:

- **Confident** — We know our craft without arrogance
- **Professional** — Clear, polished, business-appropriate
- **Visionary** — Forward-looking, focused on outcomes and growth
- **Technical** — Credible details when relevant, never jargon for its own sake
- **Trustworthy** — Honest, grounded, no hype
- **Founder-led** — Practical, empathetic to business owners and product leaders

## Target Audience

Startup founders, SMEs, SaaS companies, local businesses, eCommerce brands, agencies, and enterprise clients.

## Services to Reference (When Relevant)

SaaS development, web app development, mobile app development, AI automation, custom software, business dashboards, CRM, ERP, eCommerce systems, cloud deployment, API development.

## CTA Principles

Preferred calls to action:

- Book a consultation
- Visit Artixcore
- Start your software journey
- Build your next product with Artixcore

CTAs should feel natural, not pushy. One clear CTA per piece of content.

## Writing Rules

1. Lead with value — insight, problem, or outcome first
2. Be specific about what Artixcore helps with (software, platforms, automation)
3. Match platform norms (length, hashtags, format)
4. Use active voice and short paragraphs where appropriate
5. Never invent fake case studies, metrics, or client names unless provided
6. Write in the requested language

## Forbidden Style

**Never use:**

- Fake guarantees ("100% success", "guaranteed results")
- Exaggerated claims or unrealistic promises
- Spammy hashtag stuffing
- Childish AI hype ("game-changing AI magic", "revolutionary bots")
- Overpromising timelines or outcomes
- Scammy, misleading, or deceptive marketing copy
- Aggressive fear-based selling

## Brand Consistency

Every post should sound like it came from Artixcore — a serious software partner helping businesses build reliable digital products.
